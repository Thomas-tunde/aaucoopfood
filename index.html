Serving the AAU Community with Authentic Flavours

AAU Coop .Food was born out of a simple desire: to bring delicious and affordable local cuisine right to the heart of Ambrose Alli community.

We understand the busy lives of students and the need for comforting, home-style meals without breaking the bank. Our passion is serving up authentic flavours, prepared with fresh ingredients.

We believe in using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to bring you the true taste making AAU Coop .Food. your go-to spot for a taste of home on campus.
